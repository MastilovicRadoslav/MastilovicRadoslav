## 🚀 About me

I'm a Novi Sad-based AI engineer, full-stack developer, and PhD candidate in **Computing and Automation** at the Faculty of Technical Sciences, University of Novi Sad.

In **June 2026** I founded **LaunchAI**, my own sole proprietorship in computer programming, focused on building AI products end-to-end.

- 🎓 **Bachelor's GPA:** 8.95 / 10 — Applied Software Engineering, FTN Novi Sad
- 🎓 **Master's GPA:** 9.63 / 10 — Applied Software Engineering, FTN Novi Sad
- 🧪 **PhD:** Computing and Automation, Nov 2025 – Dec 2028 (in progress)
- 🏢 **Company:** LaunchAI (sole proprietorship, est. Jun 2026)
- 📍 **Based in:** Novi Sad, Serbia

## 💼 Currently building

Through LaunchAI I lead two large production applications, owning every layer — data model, backend, frontend, mobile and release:

- 🛠️ A full-stack product on **Go (Golang) + PostgreSQL + React**, with a companion **React Native** mobile app.
- 🤖 An AI-powered application built in **Python** on a **serverless AWS** cloud architecture, where I work extensively with **AI agents** and applied AI — directly tied to my doctoral research.
